## [0.1.0] - 2025-01-05

### Added

- `biden` now supports the following primitive types: `bool`, `uint`, `uint8`, `uint16`, `uint32`, `uint64`, `int`, `int8`, `int16`, `int32`, `int64`, `float32`, `float64`, `string`
- `biden` now supports slices of the aforementioned primitive types
